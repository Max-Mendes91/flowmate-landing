# FlowMate Color System (AA Compliant)

## Brand Palette

- brand-primary: #0A0A0A (text, high contrast)
- brand-accent: #5B4BFF (buttons, highlights)
- brand-accent-hover: #4A3CE6
- brand-accent-light: #ECEAFF

## Neutral Palette

- neutral-50: #FAFAFA
- neutral-100: #F5F5F5
- neutral-200: #E5E5E5
- neutral-300: #D4D4D4
- neutral-400: #A3A3A3
- neutral-500: #737373
- neutral-600: #525252
- neutral-700: #404040
- neutral-800: #262626
- neutral-900: #171717

## Semantic Colors

- success: #16A34A
- warning: #D97706
- error: #DC2626

## Accessibility Requirements

- Minimum contrast: 4.5:1 for body text
- Brand accent (#5B4BFF) + white text = AA compliant
- Neutral palette chosen for readability & contrast stability
